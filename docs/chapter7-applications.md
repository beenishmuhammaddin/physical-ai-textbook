# Applications of Physical AI

Physical AI has transformed multiple industries:

## Healthcare

- Robotic assistants for surgery.
- Rehabilitation robots for patients.

## Industry

- Autonomous robots for manufacturing.
- Quality inspection using AI-powered vision.

## Education

- Robots teaching coding, languages, and science.
- Interactive learning with humanoid robots.

## Daily Life

- Smart home robots.
- Delivery drones and autonomous vehicles.

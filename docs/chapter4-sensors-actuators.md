# Sensors & Actuators

## Sensors

Devices that detect changes in the environment and provide data to robots.

Examples:

- **Proximity sensors:** Detect nearby objects.
- **Cameras:** Capture images and videos.
- **Infrared sensors:** Detect heat and motion.

## Actuators

Components that enable movement and action.

Examples:

- **Motors:** Rotate wheels or joints.
- **Servos:** Provide precise movement control.
- **Hydraulics & Pneumatics:** Move heavy loads.
